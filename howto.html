Selecting items is straightforward and easy. After you sign-in, all of the items in your shop are automatically displayed.

You can search as you type for any item on your list

Initially your items are displayed from the oldest to the newest by 'Expires' date column.
To help you to choose wisely, you can change the display sort order; click on the 'Price' title to see your most/least expensive items, click on the 'Views' title to have your most/least viewed items on top, or click on the 'Likes' title to see your shop's most/least liked items.

Feeling lazy? Click on 'PICK RANDOM' and let Simply Auto Renewal to choose an item for you.

Images of the items you have chosen are displayed in the window below.
Review your list, if you want to make any changes click on an item image to unselect.


Scheduling is smart and flexible.
After you've selected and reviewed your items for renewal, choose the time zone (optional), set the date and the time, and click on SCHEDULE.

To set your renewals to repeat, choose how often and for how many times.

Keep track of your scheduled renewals, if you changed your mind, you can cancel individual items or cancel them all.

Simply Auto Renewal helps you to oversee your Etsy renewals spendings up-to-date per month.

Select a time zone from the list, set the date/time and let Simply Auto Renewal do the time conversion.
For example: You live in San Francisco, USA and your targeted clients are in Paris, France.
You think the best time to have fresh renewals would be August 15th around 11:10 AM.
Set the time zone to Central European Time, pick the date from the calendar and set the time.
Simply Auto renewal will calculate the time conversion and will renew your listings on the
15th of August at 11:10 AM Paris, France time.

You can add multiple Etsy stores and manage them under one Simply Auto Renewal account at only $4.00 USD per month and schedule unlimited renewals during that period.

SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL is designed to work with most popular tablets so you can schedule your renewals from anywhere and anytime.
