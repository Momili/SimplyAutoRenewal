SELECT ITEMSback to top

Click to select any of your items to schedule for renewal.

SEARCH ITEMSback to top

Search as you type for any item(s) on your list.

SORT ITEMSback to top

Change the display sort order; select 'Highest price' to see your most expensive items, select 'Least views' to have your least viewed items on top, or select 'Most likes' and see your shop's most liked items.

PICK RANDOM ITEMSback to top

Click on 'PICK RANDOM' button and let SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL to choose a random item for each renewal.

REVIEW YOUR SELECTIONSback to top

Review your selections, images of the items you have chosen are displayed in the panel below. To make any changes click again on any image to remove.

SCHEDULE ITEMSback to top

After you've selected and reviewed your items for renewal, choose the time zone (optional), set the date and the time, and click on SCHEDULE button.

SCHEDULE ITEMS TO REPEATback to top

To set your renewals to repeat, choose how often and for how many times.

CHANGE TIME ZONEback to top

Target clients around the globe, select a time zone from the list, set the date and time, SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL will calculate the time difference and will renew your listings just on time.

VIEW SCHEDULED RENEWALSback to top

Keep track of your scheduled renewals, if you changed your mind, you can cancel an individual item or cancel them all.

VIEW COMPLETED RENEWALSback to top

SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL helps you to oversee your Etsy renewals spending up-to-date for each month.

TABLETSback to top

Schedule your renewals anywhere anytime, SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL is designed to work with most popular tablets/smart phones.

MANAGE MULTIPLE SHOPSback to top

Manage multiple Etsy stores under one SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL account at only $4.00 USD per month and schedule unlimited renewals during that period.
