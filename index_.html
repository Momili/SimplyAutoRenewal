Selecting items is straightforward and easy. After you sign-in, all of the items in your shop are displayed automatically.

You can search as you type for any item on your list

By default, your items are displayed from the oldest to the newest.
To change the sort order, click on the 'Price' title to select your most expensive items, click on the 'Views' title to choose from your most viewed items, or click on the 'Likes' title to see your shop's most liked items.

Feeling lazy? Click on 'PICK RANDOM' and let SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL to choose an item for you.

Images of the items you have chosen are displayed within the frame below.
Review your list, if you want to make any changes click on an item;s image to un-select.


Scheduling is smart and flexible.
After you've selected and reviewed your items for renewal, choose the time zone (optional), set the date and the time, and click on SCHEDULE.

To set your renewals to repeat, choose how often and for how many times.

Select a time zone from the list, set the date/time and let SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL do the time conversion.
For example: You live in San Francisco, USA and your targeted clients are in Paris, France.
You think the best time to have fresh renewals would be August 15th around 11:10 AM.
Set the time zone to Central European Time, pick the date from the calendar and set the time.
Simply Auto renewal will calculate the time conversion and will renew your listings on the
15th of August at 11:10 AM Paris, France time.

Keep track of your scheduled renewals, if you changed your mind, you can cancel individual items or cancel them all.

SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL helps you to oversee your Etsy renewals spendings up-to-date per month.

SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL is designed to work with most popular tablets, you can schedule your renewals from anywhere and anytime at your convenience.

You can add multiple Etsy stores and manage them under one SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL account at only $4.00 USD per month and schedule unlimited renewals during that period.

How to Sign In?

SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL uses a Google Account to sign in.
A Google Account is your Gmail account (@gmail.com).
If you don't have a Gmail Account, you can create a Google account without Gmail using any existing email address you already have (e.g. @msn.com or @yahoo.com).

1. To sign in to SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL click on SIGN IN at the top of the page.

2. Then click the Sign In with Google button. You will be taken to a Google login page where you can sign in to SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL with your Gmail account and password.

3. Sign in into your Google account, the login process occurs on Google's site.
This is done to provide a high level of security for your credentials, and also to eliminate the need to register for another username and password.
SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL will NOT have access to your Google password.

4. To finish Google authentication, click on Allow button. Google will redirect you back to SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL.

5. In order to use SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL tool you must first grant access to your Etsy account.
Click the AUTHORIZE ACCESS TO YOUR SHOP button to start the process of connecting your shop.

6. Next, sign in to your Etsy account.

7. Then, click the Allow Access button.

8. After successful authorization you should now see your Etsy shop in SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL.
