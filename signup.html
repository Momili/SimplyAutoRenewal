how do I sign up?

STEP 1back to sign in

In order to to sign up SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL click on SIGN IN at the top of the page.

STEP 2back to sign in

Then click the Sign In with Google button. You will be taken to a Google login page where you can sign in to SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL with your Gmail account and password.

STEP 3back to sign in

Sign in into your Google account, the login process occurs on Google's site.
This is done to provide a high level of security for your credentials, and also to eliminate the need to register for another username and password.
SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL will NOT have access to your Google password.

STEP 4back to sign in

To finish Google authentication, click on Allow button. Google will redirect you back to SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL.

STEP 5back to sign in

In order to use SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL you must first grant access to your Etsy account.
Click the AUTHORIZE ACCESS TO YOUR SHOP button to start the process of connecting your shop.

STEP 6back to sign in

Next, sign in to your Etsy account.

STEP 7back to sign in

Then, click the Allow Access button.

STEP 8back to sign in

After successful authorization you should now see your Etsy in SIMPLY AUTO RENEWAL settings page.

STEP 9back to sign in

Click on RENEW at the top, your shop listings will appear automatically and you can begin scheduling your renewals.
